The Hobby

Hello, My name is Corey Scott, I consider myself a music enthusiast, I've enjoyed playing music since from about the age of ten. When i was in the navy, I decided i wanted to learn how to play the mandolin, and it never quite took off the way I had hoped.However, many years later, with the help and motivation from my wife, I decided it was time to get serious about the mandolin and I started taking lessons from a very talented musician and teacher named Jesse Pena. I enjoy playing and learning the mandolin very much, and hopefully this site will help inform and educate about an instrument I find amazingly unique.

My favorite mandolin players and influencers are : David Grisman, Sam Bush, Bill Monroe, Ricky Scaggs, and Chris Thile.

The Way of Life

Some people consider music as a way to escape from the pain of life. It gives you relief and allows you to reduce the stress. Music is a powerful therapy that will make you calm down and in the moment of joy, it will make you cheerful.Furthermore, it develops the mind and boosts your self confidence. Music plays a more important role in our life than just being a source of entertainment.

Food For your Soul

Some consider music an overall enhancement of life and a powerful source of joy and happiness. I find myself one of those people with the simliar thought... The mandolin brings much joy and excitment to my life.
